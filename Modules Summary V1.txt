________________________________________

Designed for AI enthusiasts and professionals eager to dive into the world of Agentic AI and Natural Language Processing (NLP). You will gain hands-on experience in building intelligent systems, mastering NLP techniques, developing agentic decision-making frameworks, and implementing state-of-the-art GenAI solutions.
Objectives
•	Build a solid understanding of Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques and their applications in AI systems
•	Implement and optimize NLP models for text classification, sentiment analysis, and named entity recognition using SpaCy and Hugging Face
•	Explore Generative AI (GenAI) techniques including transformers and language models
•	Develop agentic systems with multi-agent collaboration and decision-making frameworks
•	Master Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
•	Apply LangChain and LangFlow to build scalable NLP workflows
•	Build conversational agents using LangGraph and LangFlow
•	Monitor LLMs using Langfuse and LangWatch
________________________________________
Work Information
•	Python: Variables, data types, control structures, NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib
•	NLP: NLTK, SpaCy, TextBlob, preprocessing, tokenization, lemmatization
•	GenAI: TensorFlow, PyTorch, Transformers, GANs, VAEs
